Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about your free trial

How does the free trial work?

Sign up today and get instant access to 3 audiobooks of your choice, completely free. No payment required upfront. After your trial period, you'll receive 2 audiobooks monthly and access to our entire library of 10,000+ titles.

Can I cancel anytime?

Absolutely! You can cancel your membership at any time with no penalties or fees. Even if you cancel, you keep all the audiobooks you've downloaded during your membership.

What happens after the free trial?

After your free trial, your membership automatically continues and you'll receive 2 audiobook credits each month. You can use these credits to get any audiobooks from our library, plus you maintain access to all 10,000+ titles.

What types of books are available?

Our library includes bestsellers, classics, self-help, business, fiction, non-fiction, biographies, and more. We have titles from major publishers and independent authors across all genres and categories.

Do I need a credit card to start?

Yes, a credit card is required to start your free trial, but you won't be charged during the trial period. This helps us prevent abuse and ensures a smooth transition if you choose to continue your membership.

Can I listen on multiple devices?

Yes! Our app works on smartphones, tablets, computers, and smart speakers. Your progress syncs across all devices, so you can start listening on one device and continue on another seamlessly.
